Rosa van den Ende

I am a PhD candidate in Mathematical and Environmental Economics at Université Paris I Panthéon-Sorbonne and Universität Bielefeld, under the supervision of Agnieszka Rusinowska and Manuel Förster. I am an expert on network theory and complex systems in the context of climate policy. I am mostly interested in environmental allocation questions and in how to capture the concept of distributive climate justice in mathematics and economics.

Soon I will be available for a new opportunity! I am looking for a position where I can contribute to effective climate policy, whether that be in think tanks, public or international institutions.
